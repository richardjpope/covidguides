From:
HM Courts & Tribunals Service

The work of courts and tribunals was consolidated into fewer buildings at the beginning of the coronavirus outbreak, maintaining the safety of all in our buildings. From 17 July 2020, we are no longer publishing the tracker list as most of our courts and tribunals buildings are now open in line with public health advice.

Find the current status and contact details of courts and tribunals during the coronavirus outbreak

Details of the courts which have been assessed and can currently hold jury trials are detailed in the list on this page.
